THE STRATEGY

How the wheel works

01
Own 100 shares of HOOD
You need at least 100 shares to sell one covered call contract. That's the starting block.
02
Sell a covered call weekly
Pick a strike price above current price. Collect ~$200 in premium instantly. The buyer pays you for the right to buy your shares.
03
Reinvest 100% of premium
Every dollar goes back into more HOOD shares. At $106/share, $200 buys ~1.9 shares per week.
04
Watch contracts compound
At 200 shares: 2 contracts → $400/week. At 300 shares: $600/week. Each milestone accelerates the next.
